Insurance and Payment Information
 
ACCEPTED INSURANCE PROVIDERS:
- Jubilee Health Insurance
- EFU Life Assurance
- Adamjee Insurance
- State Life Insurance
- Sehat Sahulat Program (Government)
- Zong Sehat Card
- Jazz Health Insurance
- Allianz EFU
 
PAYMENT METHODS:
- Cash (PKR)
- Bank transfer
- JazzCash mobile account
- EasyPaisa mobile account
- Credit and Debit card (Visa, Mastercard)
 
CONSULTATION FEES (approximate):
- General Medicine: PKR 1000 to 1500
- Cardiology: PKR 2500 to 3000
- Pediatrics: PKR 1200 to 1500
- Dermatology: PKR 1200 to 1500
- Orthopedics: PKR 2000 to 2500
- Lab tests: Charged separately as per test
 
ADDITIONAL FEES:
- Medical certificate for work or school: PKR 200
- Medical fitness certificate: PKR 300
- Reissuance of lost prescription: PKR 100
- Late cancellation administrative fee: PKR 200
 
INSURANCE CLAIMS PROCESS:
- Bring your insurance card on day of visit.
- Pre-authorization is required for procedures costing above PKR 10000.
- Claims are processed within 7 to 14 business days.
- Co-payment applies as per your individual insurance plan terms.
- Contact your insurance provider directly for coverage queries.
 
NOTE: Fees are approximate and subject to change. Confirm exact fees at reception.
