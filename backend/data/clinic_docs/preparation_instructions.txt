Pre-Appointment Preparation Instructions
 
CARDIOLOGY CONSULTATION:
- Fast for 4 hours before appointment. Water is allowed.
- Bring previous ECG reports if available.
- Bring a complete list of current medications with dosages.
- Bring your health insurance card.
- Avoid caffeine on appointment day.
- Wear comfortable loose clothing for chest examination.
- If you have a pacemaker, inform reception when booking.
 
BLOOD TESTS (if ordered by doctor):
- Fast for 8 to 12 hours before blood draw.
- Water is allowed and encouraged.
- Bring previous test reports for comparison.
- Inform lab staff of any medications you are taking.
 
ORTHOPEDIC CONSULTATION:
- Bring any recent X-rays or MRI scans on CD or printed film.
- Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to affected area.
- Bring physiotherapy reports if you have been receiving therapy.
- Note when the pain started and what makes it better or worse.

PEDIATRIC VISIT:
- Bring the child's vaccination record (Red Book).
- Note temperature readings, symptoms, and how long they have lasted.
- Bring any previous prescriptions given to the child.
- Bring a favorite toy or snack to keep the child comfortable.
- Both parents may accompany for the consultation.
 
DERMATOLOGY APPOINTMENT:
- Do not apply heavy creams or ointments to affected skin on appointment day.
- Remove nail polish if nails are being examined.
- Bring photos of the skin condition taken at different stages if available.
- List any products you have used on the affected area.
 
GENERAL MEDICINE:
- Bring a list of all current medications.
- Note when symptoms started and how they have changed.
- Bring previous prescriptions and test reports.
- Fasting is not required unless the doctor has specified otherwise.
 
FOR ALL APPOINTMENTS:
- Arrive 10 minutes early to complete registration.
- Bring original CNIC or B-Form for children.
- Bring insurance card if applicable.
- Bring a trusted companion for elderly patients or surgical consultations.
