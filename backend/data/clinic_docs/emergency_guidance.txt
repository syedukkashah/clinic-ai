Emergency Information
 
MEDIFLOW EMERGENCY LINE: 0800-MEDIFLOW (Available 24/7)
 
CALL 1122 (Rescue) OR GO TO NEAREST HOSPITAL EMERGENCY FOR:
- Chest pain or pressure
- Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe head injury
- Heavy uncontrolled bleeding
- Suspected stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
- Severe allergic reaction (throat swelling, difficulty breathing)
- Suspected heart attack
- Poisoning or overdose
- Severe burns covering large areas
 
DO NOT WAIT FOR A CLINIC APPOINTMENT FOR ANY OF THE ABOVE.
 
WHAT MEDIFLOW CAN HANDLE URGENTLY (walk-in or emergency appointment):
- High fever without breathing difficulty
- Severe pain requiring immediate assessment
- Acute bacterial or viral infections
- Minor injuries, cuts, and wound dressing
- Sudden worsening of known chronic conditions
- Child illness requiring urgent review
- Dehydration and vomiting
 
BASIC FIRST AID (while waiting for help):
- Choking: Use Heimlich maneuver. Ask clinic staff for immediate assistance.
- Burns: Cool under running water for 20 minutes. Do not use ice or toothpaste.
- Bleeding: Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth. Do not remove cloth.
- Fainting: Lay the person flat and raise their legs. Loosen tight clothing.
- Suspected fracture: Immobilize the limb. Do not attempt to straighten.
 
NEAREST HOSPITAL EMERGENCY ROOMS:
- Services Hospital: 0.8 km, approximately 5 minutes drive
- Jinnah Hospital: 2.1 km, approximately 10 minutes drive
- General Hospital: 3.5 km, approximately 15 minutes drive
