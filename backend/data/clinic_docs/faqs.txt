Frequently Asked Questions
 
Q: How long does a typical appointment take?
A: The consultation itself is 10 to 20 minutes. Total visit time including waiting is
   typically 30 to 60 minutes during peak hours which are 10am to 12pm and 3pm to 5pm.
   Off-peak visits in early morning or late afternoon are usually faster.
 
Q: Can I get a same-day appointment?
A: Yes, for urgent cases. Book via chat or call before 10am for best same-day
   availability. Popular doctors like Dr. Ayesha Khan for pediatrics and Dr. Nadia
   Hussain for cardiology often have full schedules. Alternatives will be suggested.
 
Q: What if I need to see multiple doctors in one visit?
A: Inform reception when booking. We can schedule both appointments on the same day.
   A referral letter from your General Physician is recommended for specialist visits.
 
Q: Are prescriptions and reports available digitally?
A: Prescriptions are provided on paper at the time of visit. Digital copies of medical
   records are available upon request with 48 hours notice.
 
Q: Does the clinic have a pharmacy?
A: Yes. A fully stocked pharmacy is on the ground floor and is open during clinic hours.
   Most prescribed medications are available. Pharmacist consultation is free.
 
Q: Can I get a medical certificate?
A: Yes. Medical certificates for work or school absence are issued after consultation.
   The fee is PKR 200.
 
Q: Do you offer home visit services?
A: Home visits are not currently available. For immobile patients we recommend
   booking an urgent appointment and arranging transport assistance.
 
Q: What happens for emergency walk-ins?
A: Go directly to reception and state the emergency. Triage is done immediately.
   Critical patients are seen ahead of scheduled appointments.
 
Q: Can I see a doctor without an appointment?
A: Walk-ins are accepted based on slot availability. Expected wait is 30 to 90 minutes.
   Booking an appointment in advance is strongly recommended.
 
Q: What is the cancellation policy?
A: Cancel at least 2 hours before appointment for no charge. Late cancellations
   incur a PKR 200 fee. No-shows without notice may be charged the consultation fee.
