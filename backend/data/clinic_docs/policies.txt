MediFlow Clinic Policies
 
BOOKING POLICY:
- Appointments can be booked via text chat, voice note, phone call, or WebRTC call.
- Walk-ins are accepted based on availability. Expected wait time is 30 to 90 minutes.
- Same-day bookings are available for urgent cases when slots are open.
- Maximum advance booking window is 21 days.
- Each patient may book a maximum of 2 appointments per day.
 
CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Cancel or reschedule at least 2 hours before appointment at no charge.
- Cancellation within 2 hours of appointment may incur a PKR 200 administrative fee.
- No-show without any notice may result in full consultation fee charged on next visit.
- Rescheduling is free and can be done unlimited times.
 
WAITING AREA POLICY:
- Maximum 2 attendants allowed per patient in consultation room.
- Mobile phones must be on silent mode in the examination area.
- Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.
- Food and drinks are only allowed in the designated cafeteria area.
- Smoking is strictly prohibited on all clinic premises.
 
MEDICAL RECORDS POLICY:
- Patients may request copies of their medical records with 48 hours advance notice.
- Records are stored for a minimum of 7 years per Pakistani medical regulations.
- Digital copies of records are available via request at reception.
- Patient records are strictly confidential and shared only with treating doctors.
- Information is disclosed to third parties only with explicit written patient consent.
 
PRESCRIPTIONS:
- Prescriptions are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.
- Repeat prescriptions for chronic conditions require a follow-up visit every 3 months.
- Lost prescriptions can be reissued with a PKR 100 administrative fee.
 
PHOTOGRAPHY:
- Photography inside examination rooms is strictly prohibited.
- Photography in waiting areas is allowed for personal use only.
