Patient and Visitor Guidelines
 
VISITING HOURS:
- MediFlow is an outpatient clinic. There are no overnight stays.
- Family members may wait in the designated waiting area.
- For pediatric patients, both parents are allowed in the consultation room.
- For elderly patients, one attendant is permitted in the consultation room.
 
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS TO BRING:
- Original CNIC (National Identity Card) for adults.
- B-Form for children under 18.
- Insurance card if applicable.
- Previous medical reports, prescriptions, and test results if available.
 
RECOMMENDED TO BRING:
- A list of all current medications with dosages.
- A trusted family member for elderly patients or complex consultations.
- Reading material for waiting period during busy hours.
 
CONDUCT IN CLINIC:
- Maintain respectful behavior toward staff and other patients.
- Keep voices low in examination areas.
- Follow instructions from medical staff at all times.
- Photography inside examination rooms is not permitted.
- Smoking is prohibited on all clinic premises.
 
ACCESSIBILITY:
- Wheelchair ramp is available at the main entrance.
- Elevator provides access to all floors.
- Accessible washrooms are available on every floor.
- Priority seating is reserved in the waiting area for elderly and pregnant patients.
- Inform reception if you require mobility assistance.
 
PARKING:
- Free parking in basement for first 2 hours.
- PKR 50 per hour after the first 2 hours.
- Disabled parking bays available at ground level entrance.
